A 20-hour course I created and lead at ESTACA (5th-year ecomobility track) to prepare engineers to work with AI on real mobility problems. The course mixes lectures on AI agents, standards, and data infrastructure with practical assignments — students ship working artefacts by the end. One session is the Vibe Coding workshop, where they pair with an AI assistant to prototype a mobility feature end-to-end.

Full syllabus, slides, and workshop materials available via [the AI & Mobility page](/ia-mobilite/).
