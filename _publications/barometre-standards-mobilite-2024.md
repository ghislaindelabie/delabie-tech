Annual open-standards barometer co-authored with Julie Braka, published by La Fabrique des Mobilités. Maps adoption and maturity of data / service / business-model standards across French mobility operators and local authorities — tracks what has taken root, what has stalled, and what's missing for the next cycle.

Part of the [MaaS open-standards programme](/case-studies/maas-standards/).
