Contribution to France's digital-and-data roadmap for ecological planning, led by the SGPE. I was coordinator, rapporteur, and principal author of the *Se déplacer* (Getting around) chapter, in close collaboration with DGITM at the Ministry of Transport. The chapter lays out how digital infrastructure and open data become levers for decarbonizing mobility.
