{%- comment -%} Language switcher — renders one entry per configured language. Design: - The active language renders as a non-link with class="active". - Other languages that have a translation render as with hreflang. - Other languages without a translation render as disabled with a tooltip. Labels are the language's native name (French, Français, Deutsch, …) not country flags. Flags-for-languages is a W3C-flagged anti-pattern (one language, multiple countries; one country, multiple languages; emoji flag rendering is inconsistent across OSes). Addresses [REVIEW-9 @ 07:01]. The switcher is skipped entirely on pages where translation-linking doesn't make sense (missing `ref`, or auto-generated archive/category/tag pages whose language is encoded in the URL prefix). Addresses [REVIEW-9].
